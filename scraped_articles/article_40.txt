Happy Hartalika Teej 2024: QUOTES

Haryali Teej, also known as Hartalika Teej , will be celebrated today, September 06. It is a revered festival that commemorates the divine union of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. On this day, women observe a fast and adorn themselves in vibrant sarees or lehengas, particularly in shades of red and green, to honour the spirit of Teej. They offer prayers to the idols of Goddess Parvati and Lord Shiva, seeking blessings for their husbands' and families' longevity and well-being.Unmarried women also participate in the fast, praying for a suitable life partner.
