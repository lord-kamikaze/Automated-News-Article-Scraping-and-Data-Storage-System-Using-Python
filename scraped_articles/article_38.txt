Title: Cristiano Ronaldo nets 900th career goal to become...
Author: 
Publish Date: None
Content:
Cristiano Ronaldo scored his 900th career goal in Portugal's Nations League match against Croatia. The milestone came in the 34th minute from a cross by Nuno Mendes. With this achievement, Ronaldo now extends his lead at top of the list of goal scorers for both club and country. Argentina legend Lionel Messi is second with a tally of 859 goals in his illustrious career.
