Title: Kamala Mills Fire: Major fire at multi-story building at Kamala Mills in Mumbai
Author: 
Publish Date: None
Content:
The fire spread to the 5th, 6th, and 7th floors around 6.29am

MUMBAI: A major fire broke out in a 15-story building in Mumbai's Kamala Mills area on Friday morning.The fire spread to the 5th, 6th, and 7th floors around 6.29am. At least eight fire brigade teams have been deployed at the site in the Lower Parel (West) area to douse the blaze.Firefighters are currently using eight fire engines and a turntable ladder to combat the blaze.They are also working to access locked offices from the 2nd to the 14th floors using chisels and hammers .
